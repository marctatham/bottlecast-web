My Data FAQ

What can I do with my data on BottleCast?

You have control over your data on BottleCast. Here's what you can do:

  • Request Data Download: You can soon request a download of all your data from our platform, including your messages and usage statistics, through the account section of the BottleCast application.
  • Request Data Deletion: If you no longer wish to have your data on our platform, you can soon request the deletion of all your data, including your account and messages, through the account section of the BottleCast application.

How can I request a download of my data?

In the interim, while these features are under development, please follow these steps:

  1. Email your request for a data download to marctatham@gmail.com.
  2. We will process your request manually and provide you with a download link once your data is ready.

How can I request the deletion of my data?

In the interim, while these features are under development, please follow these steps:

  1. Email your request for data deletion to marctatham@gmail.com.
  2. We will review your request and, if it meets our criteria, we will permanently delete all your data from our platform.

What happens to my data after deletion?

Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. We will remove all your information, including messages and account details, from our system.

Have more questions?

If you have any more questions or concerns about your data on BottleCast, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to help!
