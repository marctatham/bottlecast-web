Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 September 2023

We, BottleCast, are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect various types of information, including:

  • Personal Information: Email address when not using anonymous sign-in.
  • Usage Data: We collect statistics on how frequently each message has been viewed, whether the "place a message in a bottle" feature was used, as well as general usage metrics such as time spent using the application, etc.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: No cookies are used; usage statistics are anonymized.

2. Use of Information

We will use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Improve our products.
  • Identify usage patterns to enhance user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We will not share your personal information with third parties, ever.

4. Your Choices

You have the right to:

  • Reach out to us to request your data.
  • Request us to delete your account as well as any messages you may have created on the platform.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. All data is secured and encrypted in transit.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and any changes will be posted here.

7. Contact Us
